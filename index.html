Haoran Liu

I work at The Chinese University of Hong Kong, Shenzhen (CUHK-Shenzhen) as a research scientist now.

I received the Master degree from Huazhong University of Science and Technology where Prof. Huijin Fan was my supervisor, and Bachelor degree from China Three Gorges University.

I won the National Endeavor Scholarship twice and won the China National Scholarship in 2018. In particular, I was the " Representative of 2019 National Scholarship Winners " (100 in china) in 2019, which published the People's Daily. That same year, I won the Ten Prominent Youths of China Three Gorges University, and enter Huazhong University of Science and Technology by postgraduate accommodation.
